About Us

Happy Hostel is a family business that has been home for travelers and backpackers here in London for the past 17 years. Our founder intended to create a place which will be more than just a place to stay in a big city. Following his passion, he has created a Happy Hostel, where every guest is welcomed with a warm and friendly atmosphere. The hostel offers a variety of amenities such as a home-made breakfast, hot shower, and a clean bed.

We are waiting for you here at Happy Hostel, your home in the heart of London!

Getting Here From the Airport

Getting Here From the Airport

Take the Stansted Express to Liverpool Street Station, from Liverpool Street Underground Station take the Central Line (Central Line towards West Ruislip) to Tottenham Court Road Station.

Gatwick Airport

Take the Gatwick Express to Victoria Station, from Victoria Underground Station take the Victorian Line (Victoria Line towards Walthamstow Central) to Oxford Street Station.

Heathrow Airport

Take the Heathrow Express to Paddington Station, from Paddington Underground Station take the Bakerloo Line (Bakerloo Line towards Elephant & Castle) to Oxford Street Station.
